How to Play

The instructions to play Rock Paper Scissors:

  • The game is played between two players: you and the computer.
  • Each player chooses one of three options: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  • The rules are simple:
    • Rock beats Scissors.
    • Scissors beats Paper.
    • Paper beats Rock.
  • The player who makes the winning choice earns 1 point.
  • If both players choose the same option, it's a tie.
  • The game continues until all rounds are played.

Good luck and have fun!
